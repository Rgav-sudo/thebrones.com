About The Brones

Formed in September 2024, The Brones are a rising Alternative/Metal band from Louisiana and Arizona. Founded by lead vocalist Caleb Gavlas, the band includes Layne Olivier, David Gavlas, Evan G, and manager Trae.

Together, they craft metaphor-rich music that channels raw emotion and real friendship.
