The Brones

Alternative & Metal music from Louisiana and Arizona

About the Band

Formed in September 2024, The Brones are a rising Alternative/Metal band making waves from Louisiana to Arizona. Founded by music writer and lead vocalist Caleb Gavlas, the band is built on friendship, passion, and a love for raw, expressive sound.

With guitarists and vocalists Layne Olivier, David Gavlas, Evan G, and band manager Trae, The Brones create music that turns unlikely ideas into powerful metaphors. They write, perform, and pour their hearts into every song — hoping to connect, inspire, and rock a few stages along the way.

Band Members

Layne Olivier – Guitar, Vocals
